Welcome to the Ace of Pentacles

This site is for the seeker of truth and insight with the magic of Tarot.

Our website allows you to choose between two tarot card spreads for your reading.

Choose your spread based on the question you would like to have answered.

Each spread is created from a random shuffle of the deck with the full 78 card spread.

It’s time to connect with your intuition and create your future. Pick your spread and Make magic, my friend! 

Past Present Future Spread

This type of spread is for those seaking insight into how a situation has progressed or regressed over time.

This spread gives you an idea of where you have been and how that effects where you are going in life.

Click the button below to get a past, present, future reading.

Celtic Cross Spread

This spread is a common spread used when you have a specific question you need answered.

It will take you step by step through the different aspects of your question.

The spread will deal with one issue at a time with each card dealing with each issue related to your situation.

Think of the question you need answered and click the button to get your reading.

Horoscope

If you are just not ready to use Tarot you could also check on your Horoscope.

Horoscopes give you information on your present situation depending on your Zodiac.

Your Zodiac sign is specific to your date of birth.

Enter your birth day, click the button, and make magic, my friend!
